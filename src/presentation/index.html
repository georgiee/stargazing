Constellations

Pareidolia?

random data = shapes
Do you know what's Pareidolia? It's the psychological phenomenon to see or hear things in random data.
And people spot really weird things in objects as you can see here. But wait, why am I telling you all this?
What is a Constellation?
Shouldn't we ask for the question what is a constellation ? Yes we should of course.
Constellations are figures people see in the sky formed by a few stars.
Star Pareidolia!
It's like looking in the night sky
and then you start seeing figures
just by connecting the stars with lines in your head.
Today there are exactly 88 star constellations. No more, mo less. And we want to build some of them with SVG & CSS. Let's get started.
